# EUV Mask Defect Detection Market Overview

EUV mask defect detection equipment refers to the specialized tools and systems used to identify and analyze defects on Extreme Ultraviolet (EUV) masks. EUV masks are critical components used in EUV lithography, a cutting-edge semiconductor manufacturing technology. These masks contain the patterns that are projected onto the silicon wafer to create the intricate circuitry of microchips.
